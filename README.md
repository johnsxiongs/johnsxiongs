# John Xiong

I build QuantAI, an AI investment research assistant for people who follow stocks, crypto assets, market themes, watchlists, and investment theses across the web and iPhone.

Based in Shenzhen. Focused on AI product systems, market research workflows, and shipping useful software end to end.

## What I Am Building

[QuantAI](https://github.com/johnsxiongs/QuantAI) helps investors and market watchers turn scattered information into clearer research threads.

It is designed for questions like:

- What changed in this stock, crypto asset, or market theme?
- Which signals matter, and which ones are noise?
- What assumptions does this investment thesis depend on?
- How should a watchlist or thesis evolve as new evidence appears?

QuantAI is research software. It is not a broker, an automated trading bot, or personalized financial advice.
